As a child I loved watching my father do his work, he was a network specialist and his teachings started my love for technology. I began at a very young age of 9 working with my father on broken computers, virus infected systems, and his most prominent job of all, networking large systems such as schools. From there my love for computers only grew stronger. My goal is to introduce you to a friendlier side of computers and make them work for you.
